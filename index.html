Our Story

How We Met

Ours is a story of fate, friendship and an abundance of love. As a financial consultant, Koko travels for work on a consistent basis and in 2022, while on a travel engagement in Raleigh NC, a mutual friend of theirs (Nkem) invited us (seperately) for her birthday in Miami, FL. Koko's weekly travel schedule did not permit this trip, but she accepted the invite and updated her travel plans to be at Nkem's birthday celebration. Koko made travel plans to arrive on Saturday evening (April 23rd). As fate would have it, Koko missed her original flight and was immediately rebooked for the next flight leaving that evening. Upon arriving, Nkem introduced the birthday crew and Koko's eyes caught a familiar face. Prior to this, Nkem has mentioned that two guys would be on this trip (Inyene included). Koko and Inyene first crossed paths in 2019 when Nkem introduced him as a Software/IT person to build our business website. After their first introduction in 2019, Inyene invited Koko to his Friendsgiving in the same year and she invited him to her birthday in 2020 (amidst the COVID-19 pandemic). They remained acquaintances until that fateful meeting in Miami on April 23, 2022.

Unknown to her, Inyene had been asking Nkem about Koko since their first meeting in 2019 but he was unavailable to make his intentions known. However, once in Miami, let’s just say Inyene could not wait to loud those intentions to whoever cared to listen. The guys arrived on Friday and explored Miami (South Beach) but Inyene refused to entertain anyone until Koko arrived. We later learned that Inyene confirmed his travel immediately after he learned that Helen (Koko) MAY be coming. Later that night in a Miami bar with the rest of the birthday crew, As a gentleman tht he is, Inyene asked Koko what she wanted to drink. And as he gave her the drink, he expressed not only how much he was interested in her, but also how long he’s been interested. She shrugged off his admiration to the drinks but his admiration became clear to Koko when one of Nkem’s friends told Inyene that she had someone for him and he boldly replied saying, “Don’t worry about that, the person I like is sitting right next to you.” We later learned that prior to this trip, Inyene mentioned to his parents that in addition to celebrating Nkem, there was someone (who may be on this trip) that he likes and was looking forward to meeting. And his mum responded, beckoning on him to "go have fun" and "to keep an open mind". Little did they know that Saturday night in Miami was the beginning of something unexpected and beautiful.

Sunday was Jet Ski and Beach Day, and we both had an amazing time. Inyene and Koko talked more and realized how much they had in common. They learned that his brother attended Loyola Jesuit College like she did, and they also learned that they had unknowingly been messaging each other on IG for months prior. Miami revealed that Inyene knew way more about Koko than she realized.

Birthday festivities ended for Koko and Inyene on Sunday night because they had early morning flights on Monday. They rode in the same Uber to the airport and talked some more. Inyene flew back to Washington DC, and Koko flew to Raleigh, North Carolina. They talked every day and each time, Inyene reminded Koko of his intentions, even though she still did not take him seriously. Two weeks later, Koko told Inyene that she did not see anything beyond friendship between them. And to her surprise, Inyene made it evident that he was ready to be in her life in any capacity that she was comfortable with, even if it was just friendship. Their friendship continued to grow and shortly afterwards, Inyene invited Koko to a weekend in DC and she obliged him.

Koko's birthday was on the horizon, and she decided to travel to West Palm Beach, FL. Without giving it much thought, she invited Inyene, and he was all in. Inyene and Koko bonded on the flight to West Palm. They talked about their families, their upbringings, tales of moving to the US and navigating their lives here, their shared values, their interests and more. They celebrated Koko's birthday together and returned to DC. At this time, Koko was on another travel engagement in Indiana/Chicago. On travel engagements, Koko could either travel home or travel someone to her location, so she invited Inyene and that was the weekend when they went on their first date. Koko teased Inyene that he had to take her on 5 amazing dates before she gave him an answer. Inyene planned their first date, and Koko was just blown away by his attention, his intention, and his empathy. Their first date went beautifully, and it was then that she knew.

They were officially dating and Inyene had already started planning their second date. During the July 4th holiday, as they were driving somewhere, Inyene received a call from his mum. He had promised to help marinate the meats so he asked Koko if she was comfortable with riding along with him to his parents so he could complete the task and return to his place afterwards. With an open mind, Koko obliged Inyene and his parents welcomed her with open arms. Koko later learned that this visit sealed the deal and that she had already become a permanent part of Inyene's life without even knowing.

Inyene planned their second date for July 15th in DC. This date was special to Koko for so many reasons. It is her dad’s birthday, and this was the second birthday that was spent without him. That morning, Koko went to run errands while keeping the special evening in mind, only to return home to the most beautiful bouquet of sunflowers. The card on the flowers read: “Next To You Is Exactly Where I Want To Be, Every Single Day, Mon Coeur! Kingsley.” Words could not describe her emotions that day, but it felt like God was steering the wheel. Throughout the date, Koko kept Inyene on edge by saying that her answer was dependent on the date's outcome even though she already knew. They walked back to the car reeling from this amazing date and in front of the car, Inyene asked her again and she said “Yes, I’d love to be your girlfriend”. It is safe to say that Koko knew her answer from date #1 and that she did not need 5 dates to decide or confirm how she felt towards Inyene.

Inyene lived in Washington DC while Koko lived 50mins west of DC which implied commuting to visit each other. During this time, they had nursed the idea of living together and Koko shared that she had always wanted to live in DC for a year. With Inyene’s lease reaching expiration, it was perfect timing to research and tour apartments in DC. Inyene took Koko to all the apartment tours which left them torn between 2 apartment options. On one of our last tours, Inyene blurted out that he was interested in moving to Ashburn. This impulsive idea turned out to be a blessing in disguise for Koko and Inyene.

Koko's mum visits the US every year from Nigeria and splits her time between LA and VA. When she visited Koko in September, Inyene and her clicked right away. Koko shared that they were planning to move in together and as mother's do, she asked about their intentions, advised Koko, and asked that she prayed about it. Inyene started moving out towards the end of October, and on November 1st, they began living together. They spent thanksgiving celebrating Inyene’s mum’s 60th birthday, where their relationship was further solidified by all the introductions to friends and family in attendance.

Koko was unable to travel to Nigeria in December 2022, so they planned to spend the Christmas holiday in Puerto Rico. Basking in the warmth and tranquility of Puerto Rico, they did not want the vacation to end. They welcomed 2023 with gratitude, commitment, and renewed love for each other. Later on New Year's Day, they drove to Inyene’s parent’s home to continue the celebration. Inyene later shared that his parents were asking when he would propose to Koko and curious of his response, Inyene reponded saying it will happen soon, "within the year".

Each time the proposal topic arose, Inyene said he would only share that it would happen before my birthday in June 2023. In January, Koko had begun consulting locally and the hours got intense very quickly. No matter how late she worked, Inyene still drove to pick her up from the train station, night after night. As a treat for both of us, Koko reserved tickets for them to experience (for the first time) a Shen Yun Performance at the Kennedy Center in Washington, DC. The performance was simply breathtaking, and was experienced with the best company.

The Proposal

Fast forward to April 2023, shy of a month from Koko's birthday, coupled with crazy work hours, Inyene thought they were overdue for another date. Koko was so excited for it that she looked up the venue, their menu and selected exactly want she wanted to eat. Inyene mentioned that they should dress up and there will be pictures. Koko simply wanted to enjoy the date and thge food so whatever Inyene suggested was totally fine. He already made all the reservations for April 30th. The day began and we completed chores and got ready for brunch. As time for brunch drew nearer, Koko told Inyene that there was no time to get her nails done and he said that he had called and shifted the time, meaning she had time to finish her nails (this should have been a give-away, but she was oblivious). Inyene insisted on helping with her press-on nails and once they finished, Inyene drove to the brunch location. Upon arriving, Koko asked Inyene to drop her in front of the restaurant so that they do not miss their reservation. He calmly insisted that they both walk in together. It was a slightly chilly day with some rain but nothing was coming between this brunch and Koko, so they walked in together with Koko walking a little bit ahead, protecting her face from the precipitation. As she entered the restaurant, walking past the front desk, her jaw dropped in excitement and pleasant surprise. Koko's eyes beheld each of her closest female friends and some of his as well. It was at that point that she realized what was happening. In amazement and utter disbelief, she turned around to question Inyene, only to find him on one knee asking Koko to be his wife. All the emotions laid bare and right in that moment, Koko said the easiest “YES To Forever” to her best friend, just 9 months after saying "YES" to becoming his girlfriend.
